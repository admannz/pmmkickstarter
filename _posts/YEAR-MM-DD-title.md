# Why Your Positioning Isn't Working

Startups often confuse **taglines** with **positioning**. 

A tagline is a catchy phrase. Positioning is the internal logic that defines who you are, who you serve, and why you matter.

## The Common Mistake

Most founders try to appeal to everyone.
> "If you try to market to everyone, you market to no one."

### Steps to fix it:
1. define your best customer.
2. List their specific pain points.
3. Map your features to those pains.
